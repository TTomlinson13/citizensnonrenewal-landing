Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when Citizens doesn't renew my policy?

You'll receive a notice giving you 45-90 days to find replacement coverage. Without new coverage, you'll be uninsured — which could violate your mortgage agreement.

Will my rates spike after leaving Citizens?

Not necessarily! Many homeowners find private market rates are competitive with or even lower than Citizens, especially for newer homes or those with updated roofs.

Can I appeal a Citizens non-renewal?

In most cases, Citizens non-renewals are part of their depopulation mandate and cannot be appealed. Finding quality replacement coverage quickly is your best option.

How long does it take to get new coverage?

With Tomlinson & Co, we can typically get you quoted and covered within 24-48 hours. Don't wait until the last minute!

What information do I need to get a quote?

Just your property address, year built, roof age, and current premium. Our agent handles the rest!
